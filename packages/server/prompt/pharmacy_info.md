## âš ï¸ Important Medication Safety Notes

**Always tell your pharmacist about:**
- All medications you're taking (including OTC, vitamins, supplements)
- Any allergies (medication, food, or other)
- If you're pregnant, breastfeeding, or planning pregnancy
- Any medical conditions you have
- Previous bad reactions to medications

**Never:**
- Share prescription medications with others
- Take expired medications
- Mix medications without consulting a pharmacist
- Stop taking prescribed medications without medical advice

**Storage Tips:**
- Keep medications in original containers
- Store in cool, dry place (unless refrigeration required)
- Keep out of reach of children and pets
- Check expiration dates regularly
